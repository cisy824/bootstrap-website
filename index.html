About us

Smiley's Puppy School is founded by a group of Media Arts and Design students from James Madison University. We are devoted to provide individualized training sessions for your dogs because we believe that every dog has its unique traits and habits.

What you can expect:

  • Hand-made, healthy snacks provided to keep your dogs happy while learning
  • Learning session based on Conditioning & Reinforcement theories
  • Certificates and graduation ceremony available

Services we offer

We offer puppy traning sessions, adult dog training classes, daycare and boarding services. Online sessions are also available!

If you are interested in booking a service, please come to our store and fill out the form about your fluffy friend, and you are more than welcomed to bring them with you!
